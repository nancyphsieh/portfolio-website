Project:
Calendar App

The calendar app that’s right for you depends on your needs and lifestyle. This is an app for those who need notes to be linked to their daily calendar/schedule. Rather than jam this calendar app with features, To Do keeps the calendar design minimal. It is has two core functions: Show upcoming schedule with attached notes. The best calendars aren’t just functional. This calendar was designed to simplify and organize our busy day, and make it a joy to use.

Design Process:
Research about Calendar apps showed two core functions: They show your upcoming schedule and remind you of important events. The best calendars, however, aren’t just functional. They give you clarity about how you spend your time, and a few rare finds are even a joy to use. Users wanted easy. Calendar apps are easy to use when they require minimal clicking and menu exploration. They should be intuitive and not require a lot of tending. Simple to share. Sharing features, such as the ability to give other people access to your schedule, can be a huge help.
