Project:
Recipe Web App

No one likes to cook the same meal every day. However, food allergies, intolerances, and health conditions require us to cook to stay safe and healthy. It’s not only tough to come up with inspiration for an exciting new meal idea every single day, it’s even more challenging when we have to substitute ingredients to accommodate our diets. Kitchen Table was created and curated by chefs and dietitians. There’s something here for every diet here.

Design Process:
Research about recipe websites indicated an increased trend of users with dietary needs and frustration with online recipe websites. Popular recipe websites had clean and modern layouts. They offered a large database of recipes with filter options. Creating profile accounts offered more personalization and sharing capabilities. Recipe sites that were too busy and limited in their filter options were frustrating, and users didn’t trust their recipes. After identifying the main components necessary for the best online experience, design iterations and prototypes were explored. Elegant typography, simple line iconography, with an earthy color palette brought warmth and heartiness to an identity based on feeding our bodies and spirits.
