Project:
Online Plant Shop

Green+House is an online plant shop who believes plants make us happier, healthier, more efficient and boost our creativity. Plants even clean the air people breathe. But for people part who live in the modern city, more and more time is spent indoors. So why not bring the outdoors in? And make it super easy.

Design Process:
Research about online plant shops indicated a trend in plant sales. Competitive online plant shops showcased plants, including planters, care instructions, and refund policies. Helping customers choose plants, as well as detailed plant filters helped minimize time and frustration. After identifying the main components necessary for the best online experience, design iterations were explored and prototyped. Bold typography, simple line iconography, and various shades and tints of green introduced a modern twist and freshness to the identity. A new logomark was created to reflect the symmetry of the natural environment around the home.
